What it covers

Building permits (bygglov), ROT/RUT tax deductions, F-skatt, reverse VAT, staff ledger (personalliggare), roofing, facades, kitchens, bathrooms, insulation, plumbing (VVS), electrical, ventilation, heating systems, hidden defects (dolda fel), contractor verification, contracts (ABS 18, AB 04), work environment (AFS), costs, BBR, PBL, energy declarations, dispute resolution, land and demolition permits, shore protection, radon, insurance, inspections, warranties, solar panels, heat pumps, fireplaces, neighbour disputes, decks and fences, property boundaries, trade certificates.
