Attefallshus & Friggebod

What is the difference between an attefallshus and a friggebod?

Friggebod: max 15 m², requires no permit or notification. Attefallshus: up to 30 m², exempt from permit but requires a building notification to the municipality. An attefallshus may be used as a permanent residence; a friggebod may not.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 §

How large can an attefallshus be?

Max 30 m² building area, ridge height max 4.0 m. Placed at least 4.5 m from the property line (unless neighbours approve closer). May not be placed in a shoreline protection area without dispensation.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 a §

Do I need to notify the municipality of an attefallshus?

Yes, a building notification is required before construction starts. The municipality issues a startbesked. No building permit is needed, but all BBR requirements (energy, accessibility, fire) apply.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 a §

What does an attefallshus cost?

Turnkey 30 m² attefallshus: 400,000-900,000 SEK including foundation, construction, electrical and plumbing. ROT (renovation tax deduction) does not apply to new construction of an attefallshus, but it can be combined with a house kit for lower cost.

Sources: Branschindex 2026

Can I have two attefallshus?

No. Per property, only 30 m² total attefallshus area is permitted (can be split across multiple buildings, e.g. 20 m² + 10 m²). Together with a friggebod (15 m²), a total of 45 m² permit-exempt building is possible.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4-4 a §§

May I place an attefallshus closer than 4.5 m from the neighbour?

Only if the neighbour gives written consent to the placement. The consent must be submitted to the municipality with the notification. If the boundary is against a street, railway or public area, the municipality's approval is required instead.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 a §

Can I build an attefallshus on farmland or natural land?

Only if the land is designated residential in a detailed plan or if a förhandsbesked allows it. Outside a planned area, a preliminary ruling or a shoreline protection dispensation is often required, which can take months.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 31 §

What is an attefall extension?

An extension to an existing single-family home, max 15 m² gross area. Permit-exempt but requires notification. May not be higher than the main building and must be placed at least 4.5 m from the property line.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 b §

Can I convert an attic as an attefall?

Yes, converting up to two apartments in a single-family home is permit-exempt (known as attefall conversion), but requires a building notification. The BBR requirements (accessibility, fire, ventilation) apply in full.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 c §

Does ROT apply to an attefallshus?

No, new construction of an attefallshus does not qualify for ROT (renovation tax deduction). ROT only applies to renovation and extension of an existing house. An attefall extension can however qualify for ROT if carried out on the main building.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.
