BBR & PBL (building code)

25 fact-grounded Q&A · 2026-04-17 · CC BY 4.0

Here are 25 common questions and answers in the category bbr & pbl (building code) for the Swedish construction industry. Every answer is anchored in primary law (PBL, BBR, KTjL, AFS, ABS 18, AB 04) and authoritative guidance. The data is open and free to use with attribution.

What is BBR?

Boverkets byggregler — Sweden's technical building regulations. A binding rulebook for all construction projects. Covers safety, accessibility, energy and hygiene. Current version BBR 29 (2024-2026).

Sources: BBR 29

What is PBL?

The Planning and Building Act (SFS 2010:900). Governs the planning of land and water as well as building. The basis for building permits, detailed plans, shoreline protection and accessibility.

Sources: PBL

Which BBR version applies?

BBR 29 applies from 1 July 2024. Revised every 2-5 years. Requirements apply at the time of the permit application → older permits may be completed under the BBR then in force.

Sources: Boverket

What is energy performance under BBR?

Primary energy value (EP_pet) measured in kWh/m²/year. BBR 29 sets threshold values per building type. E.g. villa ~95 kWh/m²/year. The energy declaration reports the value.

Sources: BBR 9

Does BBR require accessibility in single-family homes?

Partly. A newly built single-family home must meet accessibility on the entrance floor (entrance, WC, sleeping room, dining). Not mandatory on the upper floor. Accessibility is also important when renovating for accessibility.

Sources: BBR 3

What is fire class EI60?

Fire-separating function: retains integrity (E) and insulation (I) for 60 minutes. A common requirement between apartments and in stairwells. EI30 for smaller spaces.

Sources: BBR 5

Can I build without following BBR?

No. BBR is law. Deviations without permission or control lead to sanctions, demolition demands and insurance problems. Extremely narrow exceptions for private use.

Sources: PBL

What are BBR's moisture requirements?

BBR 6: moisture protection solutions are required, wet rooms must have a BBV-approved membrane, crawl spaces must be ventilated, basements need moisture protection. Moisture technicians work with Zaragoza AB.

Sources: BBR 6

Which BBR requirements apply during renovation?

In short: what you *change* must meet the current BBR. What remains untouched may stay old. Exception: at a 'substantial change', the whole building may need to meet modern requirements.

Sources: BBR 1

What is a 'substantial change' under BBR?

A change that substantially affects the building's safety, health or energy performance. E.g.: change of use, a large extension, complete redistribution. Triggers stricter BBR requirements.

Sources: BBR 1

What does BBR regulate?

Boverkets byggregler (BBR) are regulations and general guidelines that complement PBL and PBF. BBR regulates technical property requirements for buildings: load-bearing capacity, fire protection, hygiene, energy conservation, noise protection, safety in use, and accessibility. BBR applies at new construction and reconstruction and is binding when a building permit or startbesked is required. The latest edition is BBR 29 (BFS 2011:6 with amendments).

Sources: BBR (BFS 2011:6) · PBL 8 kap. 4 §

What is the difference between PBL and PBF?

PBL (the Planning and Building Act, 2010:900) is the framework law adopted by Parliament, governing the planning of land and water and building. PBF (the Planning and Building Ordinance, 2011:338) is the government ordinance that specifies the law. BBR are agency regulations from Boverket that make the requirements concrete. The hierarchy: PBL → PBF → BBR/EKS → municipal detailed plans.

Sources: PBL (2010:900) · PBF (2011:338)

What accessibility requirements does BBR set for dwellings?

BBR chapter 3 requires dwellings to be accessible and usable for persons with impaired mobility or orientation. The entrance must be reachable without steps, door openings must have a clear passage of at least 0.80 m, hygiene rooms must accommodate a wheelchair (turning area approx. 1.30 m). For two-storey single-family homes, the requirements apply to the entrance floor. Exemptions exist for leisure homes.

Sources: BBR 3:1 · PBL 8 kap. 1 §

When is a kontrollansvarig (KA) required under PBL?

A KA is required for most building, demolition and ground works that require a building permit, demolition permit or notification. Exceptions exist for small measures such as friggebodar, small alterations and certain signs. The KA must be certified under PBL 10 kap. 9 § and must be independent of the contractor. The building owner appoints the KA and notifies the building committee.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 9-11 §§ · PBF 7 kap.

What is a startbesked?

A startbesked is the building committee's decision under PBL 10 kap. 3 § that must be in place before construction may begin. The committee reviews whether the measure can be presumed to meet the requirements in PBL, BBR and the permit decision. Starting work without a startbesked can result in a byggsanktionsavgift under PBF 9 kap. A startbesked is also required for notification-obliged measures.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 3 § · PBF 9 kap.

What is a slutbesked?

A slutbesked under PBL 10 kap. 34 § is issued by the building committee when the building owner has shown that all requirements are met under the permit, startbesked and control plan. The building may normally not be taken into use before the slutbesked. For minor defects, an interim slutbesked can be issued. Taking a building into use without a slutbesked can lead to a byggsanktionsavgift.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 34-37 §§

What fire protection requirements does BBR set for single-family houses?

BBR chapter 5 requires smoke alarms in the dwelling, fire compartmentation between dwelling and garage (EI 30), and safe egress via window or door from each storey. Surface finishes in escape routes must be class C-s2,d0. Chimneys must meet clearance distances from combustible material. Terraced houses require fire-separating walls REI 60 between units.

Sources: BBR 5:2 · BBR 5:5

What do the BBR energy requirements entail?

BBR chapter 9 sets requirements on the primary energy value (EP_pet) expressed in kWh/m² Atemp per year, as well as the building envelope's average heat transfer coefficient (U_m). For single-family homes, the threshold is 95 kWh/m²/year with electric heating. The requirements are tightened in steps and verified by energy calculation before the startbesked, and by measurement within 24 months of commissioning.

Sources: BBR 9:2 · BBR 9:92

What is EKS and when does it apply?

EKS (Boverket's construction regulations, BFS 2011:10) are regulations on the application of the European construction standards (the Eurocodes). EKS applies to the design of load-bearing structures in new construction, extensions and reconstruction that require a building permit or notification. EKS refers to snow, wind and seismic loads adapted to Swedish conditions.

Sources: EKS (BFS 2011:10) · PBL 8 kap. 4 §

What acoustic requirements apply to dwellings under BBR?

BBR chapter 7 requires sound class C per SS 25267 for new dwellings. This means, among other things, airborne sound insulation R'w ≥ 53 dB between apartments, impact sound level L'nT,w ≤ 56 dB and installation noise LpAeq ≤ 30 dB in bedrooms. Higher classes B or A can be agreed. Requirements are verified by measurement or design to the standard.

Sources: BBR 7:1 · SS 25267

How long is a building permit valid?

A building permit expires under PBL 9 kap. 43 § if the measure has not been started within two years and completed within five years from when the decision gained legal force. Starting normally requires that the startbesked is in place and foundation work has begun. Time-limited permits can be granted for up to 15 years under PBL 9 kap. 33 §.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 43 § · PBL 9 kap. 33 §

What is a control plan?

The control plan is a document under PBL 10 kap. 6 § describing which checks are to be made, by whom, against what (e.g. BBR sections) and when. The building owner is responsible for drawing up the control plan, usually together with the KA. It is established by the building committee at the startbesked and must be ticked off and signed prior to the slutbesked.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 6-8 §§

What is a byggsanktionsavgift?

A byggsanktionsavgift is an administrative fee under PBL 11 kap. 51 § that the building committee imposes when someone breaches PBL, e.g. by building without a permit, starting without a startbesked or taking a building into use without a slutbesked. The size of the fee is set in PBF 9 kap. and is based on the price base amount and the scope of the measure. The fee can be halved if corrective action is taken.

Sources: PBL 11 kap. 51-63 §§ · PBF 9 kap.

What is required to build an Attefallshus under PBL?

An attefallshus (complementary dwelling house) is permit-exempt under PBL 9 kap. 4a § but requires notification to the building committee and a startbesked. Max 30 m² building area, max 4.0 m ridge height, at least 4.5 m to the property line (or the neighbour's consent). It must meet BBR requirements, including accessibility if used as a dwelling. A slutbesked is required before moving in.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4a § · BBR 3:1

What is the difference between ombyggnad (reconstruction) and ändring (alteration) under PBL?

Under PBL 1 kap. 4 §, ombyggnad is a comprehensive change that substantially renews the building or changes its use in a significant way. Ändring is a broader concept covering all measures that change a building's appearance or structure. For ombyggnad, BBR's requirements normally apply to the entire changed part, whereas ändring often allows lower requirements if the varsamhet (prudence) requirement under PBL 8 kap. 17 § applies.

Sources: PBL 1 kap. 4 § · PBL 8 kap. 17 §
