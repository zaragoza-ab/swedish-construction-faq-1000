F-skatt (contractor tax)

What is F-skatt?

F-skatt is Skatteverket's certificate confirming that you run a business and pay your own taxes and social contributions. It is mandatory for all companies performing contracting work. Clients can verify F-skatt via Skatteverket's online service.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen · Skatteverket

Why is F-skatt important for ROT?

Without F-skatt, the company cannot apply the ROT (renovation tax deduction). If you hire a firm without F-skatt and claim ROT, this counts as tax fraud and you bear the consequences. Always verify F-skatt before work begins.

Sources: Skatteverket

How do I check F-skatt?

Go to skatteverket.se, use the e-service "Kontrollera F-skatt" and enter the company registration number. The result shows whether F-skatt is active, dormant or revoked. Free of charge.

Sources: Skatteverket

What is A-skatt vs F-skatt?

A-skatt applies to employees (the employer withholds tax). F-skatt applies to self-employed business owners (the company pays its own tax and employer contributions). FA-skatt is a combination (used by some consultants).

Sources: Skatteverket

Can F-skatt be revoked?

Yes, if the company fails to pay tax, VAT or employer contributions. Skatteverket issues a warning first, then revokes F-skatt. The company can then no longer invoice as a business or offer ROT.

Sources: Skatteverket

Must a sole trader have F-skatt?

Yes, in order to qualify as a business and be able to invoice clients. Without F-skatt the work counts as employment, and the client becomes liable to withhold A-skatt and pay employer contributions.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen

How do I apply for F-skatt?

Via verksamt.se or directly with Skatteverket. The application is free, processing takes 1-4 weeks. A description of the business and expected turnover is required. It can be combined with other company registrations.

Sources: Skatteverket

Why does F-skatt protect the client?

Without F-skatt the client (building owner) becomes liable to withhold A-skatt and pay employer contributions for the person performing the work. This can cost an extra 30-50%. F-skatt places the full tax responsibility on the company.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen

Can a foreign company have F-skatt?

Yes, foreign companies can apply for F-skatt in Sweden if they have operations here. A Swedish personal or coordination number is usually required for the authorized signatory. The process takes a bit longer.

Sources: Skatteverket

What is an SNI code?

SNI (Svensk Näringsgrensindelning) is a code identifying the company's line of business. Construction uses codes in the 41-43 range. Relevant for reverse VAT: companies with SNI 41-43 are often classified as construction companies.

Sources: SCB
