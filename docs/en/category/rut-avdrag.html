What is the RUT deduction?

RUT (Rengöring, Underhåll, Tvätt — household services tax deduction) is a 50% tax reduction on labour costs for household-related services. Max 75,000 SEK per person per year (2026). Cleaning, window washing, garden work, IT help at home.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.

Is the RUT cap separate from ROT?

Yes, separate caps: RUT 75,000 SEK + ROT 50,000 SEK per person/year. Total maximum 125,000 SEK per person in 2026.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.

What is included in RUT?

Cleaning, window washing, laundry and ironing, ongoing garden work, snow clearance, childcare at home, moving help, IT services at home, furniture assembly, repair of white goods.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 a-b §§

Is garden construction RUT or ROT?

Ongoing maintenance (hedges, grass) is RUT. Construction work (new deck, fence, driveway) is usually ROT or standard taxation. The line can be unclear — ask Skatteverket when in doubt.

Sources: Skatteverket

Can companies claim RUT?

No, RUT and ROT are only for private individuals. Companies can deduct renovation costs as business expenses in the accounts, but not as RUT/ROT deductions.

Sources: Skatteverket

Does RUT apply to holiday homes?

Yes, if you own the holiday home. Cleaning, gardening and snow clearance at your own holiday home qualify for RUT.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.

Can my father claim RUT at my home?

No, RUT applies to services in one's own home. But if a parent lives with you or you help them: RUT can be used by the person who owns the home or is fully tax-liable there.

Sources: Skatteverket

How high is the RUT cap in 2026?

75,000 SEK per person per calendar year. Raised from 50,000 SEK in 2022. Two owners of a home can together claim 150,000 SEK per year. The reduction is 50% of the labour cost.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.

What is the RUT deduction?

RUT is a tax reduction of 50% on labour costs for household-related services under Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. It covers e.g. cleaning, laundry, simple gardening, moving services, childcare and IT services at home. The ceiling is separate from ROT at 75,000 SEK per person per year (RUT alone max 75,000 SEK). The provider deducts the reduction directly on the invoice (invoice model).

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Does moving help qualify for RUT?

Yes, moving services have qualified for RUT since 2016. It covers packing, carrying, transport and unpacking in connection with a move between homes in Sweden. Storage and transport to/from abroad are not included. 50% of labour is deducted; vehicle cost and materials are not counted. The moving firm must have F-tax and issue the invoice correctly.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Can RUT be used for home services by a relative?

RUT cannot be used if the service is performed by a relative you cohabit with or are married to. Children, siblings and parents can perform RUT services for you if they run a business with F-tax and do so on market terms. Skatteverket may challenge the arrangement if it appears to be a sham transaction.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. · Skatteverket

Which garden work qualifies for RUT?

RUT covers simple garden work: lawn mowing, weeding flower beds, pruning trees and shrubs, snow clearance on the property, leaf raking. New construction in the garden (lawn, paving, irrigation systems) does not qualify. Pruning over 4 m height counts as tree felling and may qualify if performed from the ground with a chainsaw.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Do IT services at home qualify for RUT?

Since 2017, IT services at home qualify for RUT: installation, repair, maintenance of computer, smartphone, router, smart TV and similar, plus help with email, security settings and software installation. Must be performed at the home, not remotely or in the company's workshop. The computer hardware itself is not included in the deduction.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Does RUT apply to holiday homes?

Yes, RUT applies to both permanent homes and holiday homes you own and use. The service must be performed in or adjacent to the home. Rented condominiums or rental apartments are also covered, since RUT does not require ownership (unlike ROT). The home must be in Sweden or an EEA country if you are fully tax-liable.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 14 § · Skatteverket

What is the maximum RUT amount for 2026?

Since 2021 a raised ceiling of 75,000 SEK per person per year applies to RUT. The deduction is 50% of labour, meaning labour costs up to 150,000 SEK can give full deduction. The ceiling has been separate from ROT since 2021. Spouses/cohabitants can claim 75,000 SEK each = 150,000 SEK per household per year.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 19 § · Skatteverket

Does furniture assembly qualify for RUT?

Yes, since 2021 assembly and disassembly of furniture at home qualifies for RUT. It applies to new furniture (e.g. IKEA assembly) and existing pieces. It also covers mounting shelves, lamps and mirrors where no advanced electrical or plumbing work is required (otherwise ROT). The furniture purchase itself is not deductible, only the assembly labour.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Can RUT be used for laundry and dry-cleaning?

Yes, laundry, ironing and care of textiles performed at home qualify for RUT. Laundry done at a laundromat outside the home is covered if the laundry is collected from and returned to the home — then transport and handling labour are included, but the laundromat's own costs (operations, detergent) are not deductible. The invoice must specify labour time at the home.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Does childcare qualify for RUT?

Yes, childcare at home qualifies for RUT. It covers picking up from and dropping off at school/preschool, supervision in the home, simple help with homework and meals. It must not be pure tuition or educational activity (then RUT does not apply). There is no age limit, but childcare of an adult child does not count as such.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · Skatteverket

Can RUT be used for snow clearance on the roof?

Snow clearance on the lot and walkways qualifies for RUT. Roof snow clearance can qualify for RUT if it's simpler snow and ice removal as part of property maintenance. If roof clearance accompanies repair of roof damage or is more intrusive, it may instead qualify for ROT. Safety equipment and fall protection per AFS 1999:3 are always required for roof work.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 13 § · AFS 1999:3

Can one person claim both RUT and ROT in the same year?

Yes, one person can claim both RUT and ROT in the same year, but they have separate ceilings since 2021: RUT 75,000 SEK/year and ROT 50,000 SEK/year. That is up to 125,000 SEK in tax reduction per person per year. Both deductions require that the provider has F-tax, that labour is specified separately from materials, and that payment is electronic.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap. 19 § · Skatteverket
