Extensions

10 fact-grounded Q&A · 2026-04-17 · CC BY 4.0

Here are 10 common questions and answers in the category extensions for the Swedish construction industry. Every answer is anchored in primary law (PBL, BBR, KTjL, AFS, ABS 18, AB 04) and authoritative guidance. The data is open and free to use with attribution.

Do I need a building permit for an extension?

Yes, extensions over 15 m² always require a building permit. Smaller Attefall extensions (max 15 m² on a single-family house) only require a notification. Timeline and cost vary depending on size and municipality.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 2 §

What does an extension cost?

Average extension cost: 18,000–32,000 SEK/m² (2026). Simple spaces (storage, carport) are cheaper; kitchens/wet rooms are more expensive. ROT (renovation tax deduction) reduces the cost by up to 15,000 SEK per person.

Sources: Branschindex 2026

How long does an extension take?

Building permit process: 6–12 weeks. Construction time: 2–6 months depending on size (a 30 m² extension takes around 3 months). Zaragoza AB offers turnkey extensions in the Helsingborg area.

Sources: Branschpraxis

Can I build a veranda without a building permit?

A veranda without a roof and under 1.8 m in height from ground level is usually exempt from permit. With a roof or glazed sections a building permit is required. An Attefall extension may also be an alternative if under 15 m².

Sources: PBL 9 kap.

Can I build an extension without my neighbour's consent?

Yes, if the extension complies with the detailed development plan (detaljplan) and the distance to the property boundary is at least 4.5 m (or what the plan specifies). Closer than 4.5 m requires the neighbour's written consent, or an exemption in the plan.

Sources: PBL 9 kap.

Can an extension be taller than the main building?

Rarely. The detaljplan often regulates building height. An Attefall extension may never be taller than the main building. Deviations from the plan require a plan deviation review.

Sources: PBL 4 kap.

Do I need a KA (certified controller) for an extension?

For most extensions beyond the Attefall threshold a KA (kontrollansvarig) is required. Simple Attefall extensions may be exempt. The building committee decides case by case.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 9 §

What fire requirements apply to an extension?

The extension must comply with BBR ch. 5. Normally the same fire class as the main building applies. If the extension is close to another building, a fire wall EI60 or equivalent is required.

Sources: BBR 5

Can I add a kitchen inside my garage?

Converting a garage into living space counts as a change of use and requires a building permit. BBR requirements also apply: insulation, ventilation, moisture protection, energy. An expensive but common renovation.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. · BBR

Does ROT apply to extensions?

Yes, extending an existing house qualifies for ROT (renovation tax deduction) on labour costs (30%, max 50,000 SEK/person/year). Materials excluded. Zaragoza AB handles ROT directly on the invoice.

Sources: Inkomstskattelagen 67 kap.
