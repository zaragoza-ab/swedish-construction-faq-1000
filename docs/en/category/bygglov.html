Building permit (Bygglov)

What is a bygglov (building permit)?

A bygglov is an official decision from the municipality's building committee granting permission to build, under the Planning and Building Act (PBL). It is required for new construction, extensions and certain facade changes. In Helsingborg, building permits are handled by the City Planning Administration. Zaragoza AB assists throughout the entire application process.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. · Boverket

How long does it take to get a building permit?

The statutory processing time is 10 weeks, with a possible extension to 20 weeks for complex cases. In practice: simple cases 4-6 weeks, larger projects 8-12 weeks. Cases involving deviation from the detailed plan may take 12-20 weeks.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 27 §

What does a building permit cost?

The fee is calculated according to the municipal tariff and varies considerably: approx. 25,000-50,000 SEK for a single-family home, 5,000-15,000 SEK for an extension, 3,000-8,000 SEK for a facade change. A planning fee is added when the detailed plan is less than 10 years old.

Sources: Kommunal bygglovstaxa

What drawings are required for a building permit?

Mandatory: site plan (scale 1:400), floor plan per storey (1:100), elevation drawings of all 4 facades (1:100), section drawing (1:100). For new construction, a nybyggnadskarta (new-build survey map) is also required. Drawings must be professionally produced and submitted as PDF.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 21 § · BBR 2

Can I build without a building permit?

Yes, for a friggebod up to 15 m², an attefallshus up to 30 m², an attefall extension up to 15 m², and smaller fences and walls. All except the friggebod, however, require a building notification. Shoreline protection areas often have different rules.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4-5 §§

What is a kontrollansvarig (KA, responsible supervisor)?

The kontrollansvarig is a certified person who oversees that the build meets technical requirements. Required for most permits above the attefall level. The KA must be certified by an accredited certification body under PBL.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 9 §

What happens if I build without a permit?

Unauthorised building leads to a byggsanktionsavgift (building sanction fee), typically 1-10 price base amounts (58,000-580,000 SEK in 2026). In addition, the municipality can demand demolition or restoration. The title deed can also be affected at the time of sale.

Sources: PBL 11 kap.

What is a startbesked (start approval)?

The startbesked is the municipality's approval to begin construction after the building permit has been granted. It is always required — starting without one results in a building sanction fee. It is issued after the technical consultation and an approved control plan.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 3 §

What is a slutbesked (final approval)?

The slutbesked is the municipality's confirmation that the build is completed in accordance with the permit and control plan. Without it, the building may not be taken into use. It is always required, even for simpler extensions.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 34 §

Can I appeal a denied building permit?

Yes. The appeal is sent to the Länsstyrelsen (County Administrative Board) within 3 weeks of notification. Further instances: the Land and Environment Court and the Land and Environment Court of Appeal. Leave to appeal is required in higher instances.

Sources: PBL 13 kap. · Förvaltningslagen

What is a detaljplan (detailed plan)?

A detaljplan is a legal document that regulates land use and development within a specific area. It is binding for building permits. Deviations from the detailed plan require a planning deviation review — a longer process.

Sources: PBL 4 kap.

What is a förhandsbesked (preliminary ruling)?

A förhandsbesked is a municipal review of whether a specific measure can be allowed, before you apply for a building permit. Useful when there is no detailed plan or in uncertain cases. Valid for 2 years if you then apply for a permit.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 17-18 §§

Can neighbours stop my building permit?

Neighbours have the right to submit opinions but cannot alone stop a permit. The municipality makes an overall assessment. Neighbours can, however, appeal a granted permit within 3 weeks if they are affected.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 25 §

Do I need a building permit for a deck (altan)?

A deck under 1.8 m in height from the ground and without a roof usually requires no permit. A higher deck, a covered deck, or a deck in a shoreline protection area requires a permit. Rules may vary by municipality.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 §

Can I use an attefallshus as a permanent residence?

Yes, since 2020 an attefallshus may be used as a permanent residence. The house can be up to 30 m², with a maximum ridge height of 4.0 m, placed at least 4.5 m from the property line (unless neighbours allow closer).

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 4 a §

What is a nybyggnadskarta (new-build survey map)?

A nybyggnadskarta is a detailed map of the property showing all existing buildings, utilities and elevations. Required for new construction. Ordered from the municipality; costs 3,000-8,000 SEK. Usually valid for 2 years.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 21 §

What is the energy calculation for a building permit?

For new construction, an energy calculation is required showing that the house meets BBR 29 requirements. Measured in primary energy value (kWh/m²/year). Performed by an energy consultant or building engineer. Zaragoza AB collaborates with certified energy consultants.

Sources: BBR 9 · PBL 8 kap.

What is fire protection documentation?

A description of fire protection measures: fire compartmentation, escape routes, extinguishing systems. Required for larger projects. For single-family homes usually simple, for multi-dwelling buildings extensive. Boverket's building regulations section 5 is the basis.

Sources: BBR 5

What is a tekniskt samråd (technical consultation)?

A meeting between the building owner, the KA and the building committee after the permit has been granted, at which the control plan and construction process are reviewed. Required for most projects. Leads to the startbesked.

Sources: PBL 10 kap. 14 §

Do I need a permit for a fence?

A fence up to 1.1 m without any other physical partition requires no permit. Over 1.1 m, or a plank/wall intended to screen, a permit may be required. Helsingborg often allows 1.8 m at a patio without a permit, but always check with the municipality.

Sources: PBL 9 kap. 8 § · Helsingborg
