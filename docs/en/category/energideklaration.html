Energy declaration

8 fact-grounded Q&A · 2026-04-17 · CC BY 4.0

Here are 8 common questions and answers in the category energy declaration for the Swedish construction industry. Every answer is anchored in primary law (PBL, BBR, KTjL, AFS, ABS 18, AB 04) and authoritative guidance. The data is open and free to use with attribution.

What is an energy declaration?

A document that reports the building's energy use. Mandatory on sale, rental and major renovations. Valid for 10 years. Produced by a certified energy expert.

Sources: Lag 2006:985

When is an energy declaration required?

On sale of a house, housing cooperative apartment, holiday home or multi-dwelling building. On rental of commercial premises. On extensive renovation (new heating system, facade insulation of more than 25%).

Sources: Boverket

What does an energy declaration cost?

Single-family house: 4,000-8,000 SEK. Housing cooperative apartment: 2,000-4,000 SEK. Multi-dwelling building: 15,000-50,000 SEK. Performed by a certified energy expert.

Sources: Branschindex

How long does an energy declaration remain valid?

10 years from the date. After major measures (new heat source, extensive insulation), a new declaration is recommended to document the improvement.

Sources: Boverket

What is the energy class?

A graded scale A-G where A is best. Based on the primary energy value. Modern villas are often C-E. Passive houses A-B. Older, leaky houses G-F.

Sources: BBR 9

Must I act on the declaration?

No, the declaration contains recommendations but is not binding. However, it documents the condition of the house, which can affect the sale price.

Sources: Lag 2006:985

What is the primary energy value?

The sum of all purchased energy times the energy weighting factor, divided by the heated area. Good value: <75 kWh/m2/year. Passive house: <45. The measure used for BBR energy performance.

Sources: BBR 9

Can I produce the energy declaration myself?

No, only a certified energy expert may issue a legally valid declaration. Search Boverket's register for authorized experts.

Sources: Boverket
